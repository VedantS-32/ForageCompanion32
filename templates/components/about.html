Our Technology

ForageCompanions uses a state-of-the-art deep learning model specifically trained on a diverse dataset of mushroom species from around the world. Here's how our identification process works:

1

Image Analysis

When you upload a mushroom photo, our AI analyzes key visual features including cap shape, gill structure, stem characteristics, color patterns, and texture.

2

Species Matching

The extracted features are compared against our database of thousands of mushroom species. The system calculates confidence scores for potential matches.

3

Information Retrieval

For identified species, we pull comprehensive information from our curated database, including edibility, habitat preferences, and potential toxic lookalikes.

4

Safety Assessment

Our system automatically flags potentially dangerous species and provides clear warnings about toxicity risks, even for species with moderate confidence scores.

Important Safety Notice

While ForageCompanions strives for accuracy in mushroom identification, we emphasize that no AI system is 100% perfect. Our tool should be used as an educational aid and not as the sole basis for determining edibility.

Always follow these safety guidelines:

  • Never consume wild mushrooms based solely on an app's identification
  • Always get verification from multiple expert sources
  • If in doubt, throw it out - no mushroom meal is worth the risk
  • Be aware of potentially fatal lookalikes for edible species
  • Start with small amounts when trying a new edible species
